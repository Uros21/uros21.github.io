We are Forest Association. Our main task and goal is to preserve our beautiful planet and its inhabitants, especially the animals that are least worried about it. If we continue to go this way, our planet will come to an irreversible state, where we will not be able to do anything more. Cutting down forests and rainforest threatens our forest friends and reduces their number, so there are more and more endangered species on our green planet. Waste water from factory plants destroys drinking water both for humans and animals. Let's be humane and end the new trend! Well, will we allow this to continue?
